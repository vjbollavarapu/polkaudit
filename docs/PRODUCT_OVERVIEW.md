# PolkAudit – Non-Technical Product Overview

PolkAudit helps people understand how decisions and money are managed in blockchain communities.

It shows:

- What decisions are made
- Who voted
- Where funds went
- Why spending was approved

Everything is displayed clearly in one dashboard.

## Purpose

To improve trust, transparency, and accountability.

## Users

- Community members
- Leaders
- Investors
- Auditors
- Regulators

## Benefit

Nothing is hidden. Everything is verifiable.
